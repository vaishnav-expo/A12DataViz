What is White hat chart?

The visualizations which are clear and easy to interpret for the intended audience (often the general population) are White hat visualizations. Any transformations, filtering, and computations done to the data are clearly and transparently communicated in the White hat visualization. Below is an example of White Hat visualization and reasons for being white hat visualization.

Why it is White hat chart?

  • It clearly states in the title what the chart is about.
  • It shows the correct data for the visualization.
  • It uses a proper colour which doesn't provide any misleading view.
  • It clearly states the source of the dataset used.
  • It shows correct and consistent annotations for the data.
  • It provides a tooltip interaction on mouse hover which helps understanding the data further.
